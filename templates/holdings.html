About Real Cost Basis:

The Real Cost Basis represents the net amount invested in the current position, calculated as:

Real Cost Basis = (Total Cost of All Buys) - (Cost Basis of Sold Shares) - (Realized Gains)

Or equivalently: Real Cost Basis = (Total Cost of All Buys) - (Proceeds from Sales)

This shows the actual amount of capital still invested in the position after accounting for both the cost of shares that were sold and any gains/losses realized from those sales.
